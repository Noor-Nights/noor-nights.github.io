🌟 About Noor Nights

The last ten nights of Ramadan are the most blessed nights of the year, containing Laylat al-Qadr (The Night of Decree), which is better than a thousand months.

This app is designed to help you track your nightly worship goals, discover comprehensive prophetic supplications (Jawami' ad-Du'a), and receive automated background notifications to keep you spiritually engaged during these precious nights.

Please keep the developer and all those involved in your sincere prayers.
