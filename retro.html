✨ The Blessing of Sharing Duas

Sharing a dua can uplift anyone who sees it during their day—reminding them to turn back to Allah, calming their heart, or inspiring them to make their own dua.

"Whoever guides someone to goodness will have a reward like the one who did it." (Sahih Muslim)

"When a servant dies, his deeds come to an end except for three: ongoing charity, beneficial knowledge, or a righteous child who prays for him." (Sahih Muslim)

Every dua you share may inspire someone else—and you share in their reward.
